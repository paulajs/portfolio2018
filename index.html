About

Independent hacker searching for vulnerabilities in web applications.

Challange

Create a visual identity and a website to promote and explain the hacking services available. In the future a security report platform will be part of the website aswell.

Hesehus

About

Hesehus is the largest e-commerce solution provider in Denmark.This project was part of my exam, and additional freelance work done after the exam.

Challange

Hesehus has the need to display their cases and their work process at e-commerce fairs. Usually the cases from their website are shown on a large screen, however those contain a lot of text. The cases should be shown in a manner more suitable for a fair, with less text and more visuals, while their workprocess should give an overview for new customers

Thoughts

At the fair Hesehus will be present among many other brands. To bring attention to their work, they will be shown with motion graphics videos to drag attention with visuals and movement. The videos should be an appetizer of Hesehus’ work. At the fair users can control the videos with an interface.

Nørremdegaard

Visual identity

About

Nørremadegaard is a farm on funen known for their fresh organic pork from their outdoor pigs and organic fruits grown on the farm.

Challange

Create a visual identity for their new attraction: A family course at Nørremadegård, where families can gather and make the marmalade the old fashion way using organic fruit. The course would take place at the farm in the summer.

Thoughts

Among many of Nørremadegaards strengths are their organic fruits grown on the farm, that is used in the marmalade.The focus in developing the visual identity was therefore put on the fresh organic ingredients available at the farm and the family experience that the course provides. The course is also considered a premium experience for the whole family, so the result should appeal to both parents and the children.

Inspiration from the danish countryside and farm hygge.
