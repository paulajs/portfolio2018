About

Independent hacker searching for vulnerabilities in web applications.

Challange

Create a visual identity and a website to promote and explain the hacking services available. In the future a security report platform will be part of the website aswell.

Thoughts

Initially trying to keep the hacker and security clichés at a minimum, however it may be necessary to include a few to better communicate the services available. Otherwise some inspiration is found in the hacking series of today (Mr Robot) and in general the cyber punk universe. Since the brand is not a large company with suit and ties, but a freelance hacker in a hoodie, the pop culture inspiration and a more laid back feel is suitable.

Hesehus

Motion graphics and React

About

Hesehus is the largest e-commerce solution provider in Denmark.This project was part of my exam, and additional freelance work done after the exam.

Challange

Hesehus has the need to display their cases and their work process at e-commerce fairs. Usually the cases from their website are shown on a large screen, however those contain a lot of text. The cases and their work process should be shown in a manner more suitable for a fair, with less text and more visuals.

Thoughts

At the fair Hesehus will be present among many other brands. To bring attention to their work, they will be shown with motion graphics videos to drag attention with visuals and movement. The videos should be an appetizer of Hesehus’ work. At the fair users can control the videos with an interface.

Nørremadegaard

project

Concept & Visual identity

about

Nørremadegaard is a farm on Funen known for their fresh organic pork and fruits.

challange

Create a visual identity for Nørremadegård new attraction: A family course, where families can gather and make marmalade the old fashion way using fruit from the farm.

inspiration

Inspiration from the danish countryside and farm hygge.

Inspiration from premium products and products from parents childhood.

thoughts

Among many of Nørremadegaards strengths are their organic fruits grown on the farm, that is used in the marmalade. The focus in developing the visual identity was therefore put on the fresh organic ingredients available at the farm and the family experience that the course provides. The course is also considered a premium experience for the whole family, so the result should appeal to both parents and the children.

design objectives

  • Summer in Denmark.
  • Danish farm hygge.
  • Remind the parents of family outings in childhood.
  • The desire to give own children same nice summer/family experience as they had themselves.

experimentation

Palm print development and research for logo. Symbolises marmelade and also childhood play in kitchen.

Development of visual identity. Experiments with color, form and texture
